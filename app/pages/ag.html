What it costs you when RTK drops

Real numbers, real harvest seasons

Planting overlap
A 30 cm fix instead of 2 cm = ~6 inches of overlap or skip per pass. On a 1,500-acre operation, that's $5–15 / acre in lost seed + chemistry per bad day.
$7,500–$22,500 / day / op
💧
Variable-rate spraying
VRA / VRT applicators rely on RTK to hit prescription zones. A dropped fix sprays the wrong rate on the wrong acres — wasted chemistry and yield drag carry into August.
$8–20 / acre wasted N+P+K
🌽
Harvest combine drift
Yield-monitor accuracy collapses without RTK; section-control over- and under-cuts headlands. The yield map you pay for is unreliable all season.
~3–8% yield-map error
