How To Use It

Three steps to your
first route decision

1
Plot Your Route
Click "⊕ Click map to add waypoint" in the right panel, then click anywhere on the 3D globe to place waypoints. Or type lat/lon coordinates directly in the form.
2
Select Platform
Choose your platform type from the dropdown: HMMWV, MRAP, Rotary Wing, Fixed Wing, Maritime, or Dismounted. Each maps to a GPS receiver type and criticality level.
3
Get Decision
Click "▶ Get Route Decision". The route segments color by risk level, the panel shows your go/no-go verdict, confidence score, per-waypoint table, and provenance.
4
Explore Forecast
Use the KP FORECAST timeline bar at the bottom of the globe to scrub through 72 hours. Watch TEC bands expand as Kp climbs.
