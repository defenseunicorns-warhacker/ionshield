Return on Investment

The cost of one bad call
exceeds the pilot fee

A single GPS-guided vehicle rerouting due to unplanned ionospheric degradation costs hours of mission time and fuel. A satellite uplink retransmission window costs thousands per hour of lost payload downtime. A polar flight diversion costs five figures in fuel alone.

IonShield delivers route-specific warnings before departure — not after the problem is discovered in the field. The pilot program is designed to measure your specific ROI across the 60–90 day period, documented in the final report.
